# Acme Robotics — Employee Handbook (excerpt)

## Time off
Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per year, accrued
monthly. Unused PTO of up to 5 days may be carried into the next calendar year.

## Remote work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote arrangements
require director approval and are reviewed every 6 months.

## Expenses
Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date.
Meal expenses are reimbursed up to a cap of $50 per day while travelling.
Receipts are required for any expense over $25.

## Benefits
Health insurance coverage begins after 30 days of employment and includes
dental and vision. The company matches 401(k) contributions up to 4% of salary.
Acme provides 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave for all new parents.

## Equipment
New employees receive a one-time home office stipend of $1,500 for a desk,
chair, and accessories. Laptops are company-owned and must remain encrypted.

## Security
All security incidents must be reported within 24 hours to security@acme.com.
Lost or stolen devices must be reported immediately so they can be wiped
remotely.

## Employment terms
New hires serve a probation period of 90 days. After probation, either party
may end employment with 2 weeks of written notice.
